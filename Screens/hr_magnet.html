Landing Page

The landing page showcases the core features of HR Magnet with a clean, professional design. It highlights employee management capabilities, recruitment tools, and analytics features while maintaining an approachable and modern aesthetic that appeals to HR professionals.

User Registration

A streamlined sign-up process designed to quickly onboard new users while collecting essential company and user information. The form is intuitive and professional, ensuring a smooth first interaction with the platform.

A comprehensive dashboard providing HR professionals with real-time insights into company metrics, employee statistics, recent activities, and quick access to key functions. The design prioritizes data visualization and actionable information.
